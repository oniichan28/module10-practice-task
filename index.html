Influencer gear refers to the equipment used by content creators to produce and share their work online, including cameras, microphones, lighting, and editing software. It's the tools that help them build an audience and establish their online presence. High-quality gear can enhance the visual and audio appeal of content, attracting more viewers and engagement.
